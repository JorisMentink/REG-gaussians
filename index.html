Overview

REG-Gaussians investigates how explicit regularization can improve convergence and reconstruction quality when optimizing Gaussian primitives from limited projection data.

  • Problem: sparse view and motion cause artifacts and instability.
  • Idea: add regularizers to preserve geometry and temporal coherence.
  • Outcome: improved stability and sharper structure in reconstructions.
